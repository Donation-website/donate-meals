Our Mission 🎓🍎

Since 2017, we have been committed to improving the lives of children everywhere. With a growing network of more than 25,000 volunteers across 40 countries, we work tirelessly to provide education, nutrition, and care to those who need it most.

Thanks to our supporters, we have already reached over 1 million children worldwide, giving them the chance to attend school 🎒, enjoy proper meals 🍲, and receive vital healthcare services 🏥.

Our mission is simple: every child deserves a safe childhood, opportunities for growth, and hope for the future 🙂.

How Donations Are Used 💝

Your generosity makes life-changing projects possible. Here is how contributions are used:

🍲 45% provides food programs and daily meals for children.
🎒 35% funds schools, books, and educational materials.
🏥 20% supports medical care, vaccinations, and emergency relief.

Every euro counts. From building classrooms in rural villages to delivering warm meals in refugee camps, your support transforms lives 🌟.
